How to play:

If you’re lucky enough to have “friends”, then you can play in a couple of styles: (Feel free to incorporate alcohol however you want!)

Casual: Take turns rolling infinitely until you get a phrase you’re cool with.

All-Play: Keep rolling as a group, and when a player sees a command that they’re willing to try, they volunteer to do it!

You can’t get out of it: Players take turns rolling. Each time you roll, you can choose to pass or do the task. You can roll up to five times.
